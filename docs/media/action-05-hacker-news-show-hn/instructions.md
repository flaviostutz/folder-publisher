# Publish Instructions

- Platform: Hacker News
- Goal: reach technical early adopters and gather high-signal critique
- Best publish time: Tuesday to Thursday, 14:00-16:00 UTC
- Frequency: one launch submission only; if it fails, do not repost the same copy immediately, wait at least 2 weeks and lead with a narrower use case
- Format: use the Show HN title and body; publish the first comment immediately after submitting
- CTA: do not ask for stars inside the title; keep the repo link in the body and let the first comment invite workflow feedback
- Comment plan: stay present for 3 to 4 hours after posting and answer every technical question with specifics, not marketing language
- Tracking: points, comments, click-throughs, and stars during the first 24 hours
- Quality bar: if there is no screenshot or short architecture image ready, delay publishing until there is one
